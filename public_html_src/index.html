About me

I work on transparency and accountability in machine learning with applications to security, privacy, and fairness. I work on making computers explain themselves. I want to make sure computers do not learn sad things from the sad world or remember things about us we would rather they not. This makes the world not as sad and computers not as bad. Woof woof.
